## What is a Flag?

Each challenge is designed to introduce a new concept in **cryptography**. The goal of solving a challenge is to find a hidden **flag** — a short string that proves you solved it correctly.

## Flag Format

```
crypto{y0ur_f1rst_fl4g}
```

- Always wrapped in `crypto{` ... `}`
- Helps verify you found the **correct** solution
- Submit it into the challenge form to mark it as solved

## Key Takeaways

- Flags confirm correct solutions — if the format matches, you're on the right track
- The first challenge flag is given directly: `crypto{y0ur_f1rst_fl4g}`
- Subsequent flags must be **derived** by solving each crypto challenge
